About Us

The Space Trone project is a sponsored mission under the UN Creative Works department responsible for the civilian space program, as well as aeronautics and space research. The project was established in 2016, succeeding the UN SPace Mission Committee for Aeronautics. The project is currently the biggest aerospace manufacturer and space transportation services organisation headquartered in Johannesburg, South Africa.
